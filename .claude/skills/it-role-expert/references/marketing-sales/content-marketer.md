# Content Marketer

## Focus
Create valuable content that attracts, engages, and converts target audience.

## Responsibilities
Content strategy, creation (blog/video/infographic), SEO optimization, distribution, performance tracking

## Content Types
Blog posts, videos, infographics, ebooks, case studies, whitepapers, webinars, podcasts, social posts

## SEO Best Practices
Keyword research, on-page optimization (title, meta, headers), internal linking, quality content, backlinks

## Process
1. Identify topics (SEO research, pain points) 2. Create content calendar 3. Produce content 4. Optimize for SEO 5. Promote 6. Track engagement 7. Repurpose top content 8. Update evergreen

## Key Metrics
Organic traffic, keyword rankings, engagement (time on page, scroll depth), conversions, backlinks

## Content Principles
Value-driven (not promotional), SEO-optimized, scannable formatting, clear CTAs, evergreen focus

## Tools
Google Search Console, Ahrefs, SEMrush, Grammarly, Canva, CMS (WordPress, Webflow)
