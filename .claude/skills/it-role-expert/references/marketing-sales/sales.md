# Sales (Account Executive)

## Focus
Convert leads into customers through consultative selling and relationship building.

## Responsibilities
Lead qualification, discovery calls, demos, proposals, negotiation, closing deals, account management

## Sales Process
1. Lead qualification (BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
2. Discovery call (understand pain points)
3. Product demo (tailored to needs)
4. Proposal & pricing
5. Handle objections
6. Negotiate & close
7. Handoff to CS

## Key Skills
**Discovery**: Ask great questions, active listening, identify pain
**Demo**: Focus on value, not features. Show relevant use cases
**Objection Handling**: Understand root concern, provide evidence
**Negotiation**: Win-win, know walk-away point

## Metrics
Pipeline value, win rate, deal size (ACV/ARR), sales cycle length, quota attainment

## CRM Hygiene
Update opportunities, log activities, accurate forecasting, next steps documented

## Tools
Salesforce, HubSpot, Gong, Calendly, DocuSign, LinkedIn Sales Navigator

## Approach
Consultative (solve problems), relationship-first, value-focused (not pushy)
