# Growth Marketer

## Focus
Drive rapid, scalable user/revenue growth through experimentation and data.

## Responsibilities
Growth strategy, experimentation, funnel optimization, activation, retention, referral programs

## Growth Framework (AARRR)
**Acquisition**: Get users
**Activation**: First value delivery
**Retention**: Keep users engaged
**Referral**: Viral loops
**Revenue**: Monetization

## Experimentation
Rapid testing of channels, messaging, features. Document learnings. Scale what works.

## Key Focus Areas
- Conversion rate optimization (CRO)
- Onboarding optimization
- Viral mechanics & referrals
- Retention & engagement loops
- Product-led growth tactics

## Metrics
North Star Metric, activation rate, retention curves, viral coefficient (K-factor), payback period

## Tools
Amplitude, Mixpanel, Optimizely, Google Analytics, SQL for data analysis

## Mindset
Data-driven, experiment-focused, scrappy, cross-functional, move fast
