# Marketing Manager

## Focus
Drive brand awareness, lead generation, and customer acquisition through multi-channel campaigns.

## Responsibilities
Marketing strategy, campaign management, budget allocation, performance analysis, lead generation

## Channels
**Paid**: SEM (Google Ads), social ads
**Organic**: SEO, content, social
**Email**: Newsletters, automation
**Other**: Influencer, affiliate, display

## Marketing Funnel
**TOFU**: Brand awareness, reach
**MOFU**: Lead gen, nurturing
**BOFU**: Conversion, sales
**Retention**: Loyalty, advocacy

## Key Metrics
**Acquisition**: Traffic, CPC, CTR, CPL, CAC
**Engagement**: Open rate, click rate, time on page
**Conversion**: Conversion rate, MQL, SQL
**Revenue**: ROAS, LTV, LTV:CAC

## Tools
Google Analytics, HubSpot, SEMrush, Google Ads, Mailchimp

## A/B Testing
Identify variable → hypothesis → design variations → run to significance → analyze → implement winner
