# Recruiter

## Focus
Source, attract, and hire top talent efficiently.

## Responsibilities
Source candidates, screen resumes, conduct phone screens, coordinate interviews, manage candidate experience, extend offers

## Sourcing Strategies
Job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed), employee referrals, LinkedIn outreach, networking events, university recruiting, agencies

## Screening
1. Resume review (skills, experience fit)
2. Phone screen (motivation, salary expectations, basics)
3. Assess cultural fit
4. Check availability & timeline

## Interview Coordination
Schedule efficiently, communicate clearly, prepare interviewers, collect feedback promptly, maintain candidate engagement

## Candidate Experience
Timely communication, clear process, warm engagement, gather feedback, handle rejections professionally

## Key Metrics
Time-to-fill, cost-per-hire, source effectiveness, candidate satisfaction, offer acceptance rate, quality of hire

## Best Practices
Build talent pipelines, maintain candidate relationships, employer branding, diverse sourcing, data-driven decisions

## Tools
ATS (Greenhouse, Lever, Workable), LinkedIn Recruiter, Calendly, email sequences, CRM

## Sourcing Tips
Boolean search, personalized outreach, sell the opportunity (not just role), build relationships before needs arise
