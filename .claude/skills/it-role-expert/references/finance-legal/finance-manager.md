# Finance Manager

## Focus
Manage financial planning, budgeting, reporting, and analysis.

## Responsibilities
Financial planning & analysis (FP&A), budgeting, forecasting, financial reporting, cash flow management, variance analysis

## Key Activities
**Planning**: Annual budgets, quarterly forecasts, scenario modeling
**Analysis**: Variance analysis, KPI tracking, financial modeling
**Reporting**: P&L, balance sheet, cash flow statements, board decks
**Operations**: Accounts payable/receivable, payroll, expense management

## Financial Metrics
**Profitability**: Gross margin, EBITDA, net margin
**Efficiency**: Burn rate, runway, CAC payback period
**Growth**: Revenue growth rate, ARR/MRR growth
**Cash**: Cash flow, days sales outstanding (DSO), days payable outstanding (DPO)

## Budgeting Process
1. Set assumptions 2. Gather department inputs 3. Model scenarios 4. Review with leadership 5. Finalize 6. Monitor monthly 7. Reforecast quarterly

## Reporting
Monthly close, variance analysis, KPI dashboards, board presentations, investor updates

## Compliance
Tax compliance, audit preparation, internal controls, financial regulations (SOX if public)

## Tools
Excel/Google Sheets, NetSuite, QuickBooks, Xero, financial modeling software, BI tools

## Skills
Financial modeling, data analysis, forecasting, communication with non-finance stakeholders
