# Legal Counsel

## Focus
Provide legal advice, draft/review contracts, ensure compliance, mitigate risks.

## Responsibilities
Contract drafting/negotiation, legal advice, regulatory compliance, IP protection, dispute handling, employment law

## Contract Review
1. Understand context 2. Review key terms (payment, liability, IP, termination) 3. Identify risks 4. Redline 5. Negotiate 6. Execute 7. Store

## Key Contract Provisions
**Payment**: Amount, schedule, late fees
**Liability**: Caps, exclusions, indemnification
**IP**: Ownership, licensing
**Confidentiality**: Scope, duration
**Termination**: Conditions, notice
**Dispute Resolution**: Arbitration, jurisdiction

## Contract Types
MSA, SOW, SaaS agreements, NDAs, employment contracts, shareholder agreements

## IP Protection
Trademark registration, copyright, trade secrets (NDAs), employee IP assignments, open source compliance

## Data Privacy
**GDPR**: EU data protection
**CCPA**: California privacy
Privacy policies, DPAs, breach response, vendor diligence

## Employment Law
Contracts, handbooks, wage & hour, classification (employee vs contractor), anti-discrimination, termination, leave compliance

## Red Flags
Unlimited liability, broad indemnification, unilateral changes, auto-renewal, unfavorable IP terms

## Tools
DocuSign, Ironclad, Westlaw, LexisNexis, contract management systems
