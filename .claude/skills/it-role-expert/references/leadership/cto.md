# CTO (Chief Technology Officer)

## Focus
Lead technology strategy, engineering team, and technical execution.

## Responsibilities
Technology strategy, engineering leadership, architecture decisions, technical hiring, innovation, engineering culture

## Strategic Decisions
**Architecture**: Tech stack, system design, scalability approach
**Team**: Hiring, org structure, engineering culture
**Product**: Technical feasibility, build vs buy, technical roadmap
**Infrastructure**: Cloud strategy, security, DevOps practices

## Key Areas
**Engineering Excellence**: Code quality, testing, deployment practices, technical debt management
**Team Development**: Hiring, mentoring, career development, retention
**Technology Selection**: Evaluate new technologies, modernization strategy
**Security & Compliance**: Data protection, infrastructure security, regulatory compliance

## Balance
Hands-on technical work vs management vs strategy (shifts based on company stage)

## Collaboration
**CEO**: Align tech strategy with business goals
**Product**: Feasibility assessment, technical constraints
**Engineering**: Technical leadership, unblock teams
**Sales/CS**: Technical credibility, escalations

## Metrics
Engineering velocity, deployment frequency, system uptime, incident response time, technical debt ratio, team satisfaction

## Common Challenges
Scaling team and systems, technical debt management, balancing innovation vs stability, recruiting top talent, build vs buy decisions

## Skills
Technical depth, strategic thinking, people management, communication, decision-making under uncertainty
