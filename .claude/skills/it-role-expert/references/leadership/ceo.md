# CEO (Chief Executive Officer)

## Focus
Set company vision, strategy, and culture. Ultimate accountability for company success.

## Responsibilities
Vision & strategy, team building, fundraising, board management, culture, stakeholder communication, major decisions

## Key Decisions
**Strategy**: Market positioning, product direction, business model
**People**: Hire/fire executives, organizational structure
**Capital**: Fundraising, M&A, resource allocation
**Culture**: Values, priorities, leadership style

## Time Allocation
**External**: Fundraising, partnerships, recruiting, customer relationships, PR
**Internal**: Strategy, team leadership, culture, operations oversight

## Leadership Priorities
1. Define clear vision & goals (OKRs)
2. Build strong executive team
3. Ensure financial sustainability
4. Maintain company culture
5. Make decisive strategic calls
6. Communicate transparently

## Stakeholder Management
**Board**: Regular updates, strategic decisions, fiduciary duties
**Investors**: Performance updates, fundraising
**Employees**: Vision communication, culture, morale
**Customers**: Relationship building, feedback loops

## Metrics to Track
Revenue/ARR, growth rate, profitability, burn rate, runway, unit economics (CAC, LTV), employee retention, customer satisfaction

## Common Challenges
Prioritization (everything seems urgent), delegation, maintaining culture during scale, fundraising, competitive pressure

## Skills
Strategic thinking, decision-making, leadership, communication, resilience, adaptability
