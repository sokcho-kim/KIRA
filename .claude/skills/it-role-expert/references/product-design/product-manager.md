# Product Manager

## Focus
Define product vision and decide what to build based on user needs and business goals.

## Responsibilities
Product strategy/roadmap, gather requirements, prioritize features, write specs, coordinate teams, track KPIs

## Prioritization Frameworks
**RICE**: Reach × Impact × Confidence / Effort
**MoSCoW**: Must/Should/Could/Won't have
**Value vs Effort**: High value, low effort first

## Key Metrics
**Acquisition**: MAU, DAU, conversion
**Engagement**: Retention (D1/D7/D30), feature adoption
**Monetization**: MRR, ARPU, LTV, LTV:CAC
**Satisfaction**: NPS, CSAT, churn

## Process
1. Identify problem 2. User research 3. Define requirements & metrics 4. Create PRD 5. Work with design 6. Prioritize with eng 7. Launch & monitor 8. Iterate

## Tools
Jira, Linear, Asana, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Confluence, Notion
