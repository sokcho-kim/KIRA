# Product Designer

## Focus
Blend UX and UI to solve problems end-to-end from research to visual design.

## Responsibilities
Combines UX research, interaction design, and visual design. Own full design process from problem definition to final implementation.

## Skills Combined
**UX**: Research, personas, flows, wireframes, usability testing
**UI**: Visual design, branding, design systems, animations
**Product**: Business goals, metrics, prioritization

## End-to-End Process
1. Research & discovery 2. Problem definition 3. Ideation & concepts 4. Wireframing 5. Visual design 6. Prototyping 7. Testing 8. Implementation support 9. Measure & iterate

## Key Considerations
User needs + business goals + technical feasibility = successful product

## Deliverables
Research insights, personas, flows, wireframes, high-fidelity designs, prototypes, design specs, design system contributions

## Tools
Figma (primary), Maze, UserTesting, Amplitude, Miro

## Collaboration
Close work with PM (strategy), engineers (feasibility), researchers (insights)
